Welcome to TaskBuddie



TaskBuddie is a tool that allows you to create team tasks.


You will be able to organize the corresponding activities of each member and their progress.



    Free Version

  • Create boards with 5 members.

  • Assign differentiated tasks to each team member.

  • Participation rankings.

  • Add images to your favorite tasks.

    • Premium Version

  • Create boards with unlimited members.

  • Assign a background to your favorite boards.
